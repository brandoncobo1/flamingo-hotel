About Us

Where refined
design meets
genuine warmth.

A 4-star boutique hotel in the heart of Vlorë, Albania. 500 metres from the beach. Bar, minimarket, free parking — and a team that remembers your name.

Our Philosophy

Boutique by choice.
Exceptional
by design.

Flamingo Hotel was built for guests who expect more — not in volume, but in quality. Every room is designed with care: air conditioning, flat-screen TV, minibar, private bathroom with quality toiletries. Not a single detail is an afterthought.

Our on-site bar is the perfect place to unwind after a day at the beach. Our breakfast room serves a fresh Albanian breakfast each morning — the kind that actually sets you up for the day.

We are rated 9.2 on Booking.com. We aim to earn that score again on every single visit.

What We Stand For

Built on three beliefs.

I — Quality

No details skipped.

Every room at Flamingo Hotel is equipped with air conditioning, flat-screen TV, minibar, free WiFi, private bathroom, quality toiletries and daily housekeeping. When we say 4 stars, we mean all of it — not the parts that are convenient to deliver.

II — Hospitality

Albanian warmth.
International standards.

Our front desk operates 24 hours. Our staff speak multiple languages. But more than that — they listen. Restaurant recommendation? Transfer coordination? A local secret? We are here for all of it, without being asked twice.

III — Wellbeing

Rest is part
of the experience.

Our bar and common areas are not an afterthought — they are central to what Flamingo Hotel is. Whether you arrive for a business trip or a beach holiday, we believe your body deserves to be taken care of. Soundproof walls, comfortable beds, blackout curtains: sleep is taken seriously here.
